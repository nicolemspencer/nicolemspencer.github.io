About Me

Hi, I'm Nicole. I'm a designer who dabbles in a little bit of everything. Typography and photography hold a special place in my heart, as do palm trees, plantains, disco music and dancing. I was born and raised in Miami, FL, which means I probably said "dale" once or twice before Pitbull made it cool and I actively have to stop myself from greeting people with the "Miami kiss".
